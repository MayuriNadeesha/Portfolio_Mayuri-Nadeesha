About Me

Who I Am

I'm Mayuri Nadeesha, a passionate Quality Assurance Engineer with a strong academic background in Computing and Information Systems and hands-on experience in both manual and automation testing. My journey in software quality began with a curiosity about how things work and evolved into a professional commitment to ensuring flawless user experiences.

What drives me is the challenge of finding that one critical bug before it reaches production. I approach testing with a blend of technical precision and creative thinking - not just verifying requirements but imagining how real users might interact with the software in unexpected ways.

My QA Philosophy

I believe quality assurance is more than just finding bugs; it's about being the user's advocate throughout the development process. My testing approach combines:

  • Precision: Meticulous attention to detail in test case design and execution
  • Efficiency: Leveraging automation for regression testing while maintaining smart manual testing
  • Collaboration: Working closely with developers in Agile environments to prevent defects
  • Continuous Learning: Staying current with evolving testing methodologies and tools

Beyond Testing

When I'm not testing software, you'll find me:

  • Contributing to open-source testing projects on GitHub
  • Reading about the latest trends in AI and machine learning applications in QA
  • Participating in hackathons and tech community events

I'm currently seeking opportunities to bring my combination of technical skills, academic knowledge, and passion for quality to a forward-thinking development team. Let's connect and discuss how I can contribute to your quality assurance efforts!
